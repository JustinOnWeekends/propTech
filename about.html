Our Story

Built because we nearly bought the wrong house

PropInsight started with a personal frustration — spending 4 evenings researching a single property across six different government websites. There had to be a better way.

Ireland's housing market is the most consequential financial decision most people will ever make. Yet the information infrastructure is stuck in 2005.

The data exists. The OPW publishes flood maps. An Bord Pleanála publishes planning decisions. The Property Price Register publishes every sale. An Garda Síochána publishes crime statistics. But they're scattered across different portals, in different formats, with different interfaces — and most buyers have no idea any of it exists until it's too late.

We built PropInsight to fix that. One Eircode. One report. Every data source that matters. Plain English, not government jargon. In 60 seconds, not 4 hours.

We're starting with Cork — because we know it, because the data is there, and because 61,000+ transactions in the PPR alone gives us a solid foundation. Dublin, Galway, and Limerick are next.
